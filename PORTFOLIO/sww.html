Group project
Task: Short video without any dialogue about the topic “Time”.

Our chosen topic for a short video was time, because the topic is so open and there is a lot of space to get creative. We played with the relativity of time and how it can trick our mind into believing something, that is not real. The ending of the film can be understood and interpreted differently, which was our main purpose-to wake up the sleeping creativity of the viewer. In this project i was playing the main actor’s roll, as well as edited the production and took part in the ideation.
