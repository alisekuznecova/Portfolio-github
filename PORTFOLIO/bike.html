Group project
Task: Informational or promotional video for the partner- The Copenhagen Municipality’s Bicycle Program.

We chose to target people, who has just arrived in Denmark and feel discouraged to bike in Copenhagen, because of all the new rules to learn and unfamiliar biking culture. In the video we are using banners with information of what is the right thing to do in the particular situation, in this way hoping to make the rules more memorable for viewers. Since I already experienced being in front of the camera in previous project, I wanted take a challenge this time and work as a camera operator. It wasn’t the first time I held a professional camera in my hands, but this time there were many factors, that made the task more challenging- unsteady and often unfavorable weather, motion shots and filming in a public place.
