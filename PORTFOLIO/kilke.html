Group project
Task: Redesign of the already existing website.

Our given website was about Kilkee Thalassotherapy centre-it had countless issues in terms of usability and visual appearance, which we attempted to fix. Since this was one of the first group projects, it gave a great understanding of how to clearly express our ideas to other members, how to compromise and organise productive group work. My responsibility was to collect the data from all the members of the group in one final presentation, as well as to make sure that everything is completed in time and everyone has a responsibility.
